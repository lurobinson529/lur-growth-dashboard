Discovery Call Framework
Part 1 — Open
5-10 min. Let them settle. Ask where they are right now, not what they need.
Part 2 — Diagnose
20-25 min. Surface the capacity gap. One question at a time. Never interrupt with a solution.
Part 3 — Frame
10 min. Reflect what you heard. Name the gap calmly. Do not offer solutions yet.
Part 4 — Close
5-10 min. One clear next step. Proposal, follow-up, or leave open. No pressure.
Send proposal if:
Capacity gap confirmed. Budget exists. Timeline within 60 days. They expressed readiness.
